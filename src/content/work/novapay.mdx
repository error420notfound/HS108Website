## The Challenge

NovaPay was operating in an overcrowded fintech market with no clear differentiator. Their existing brand felt like every other payments app — generic iconography, safe blue palette, forgettable typography. Conversion was struggling and enterprise clients weren't taking them seriously.

## Our Approach

We started with a two-week discovery phase, interviewing their top 20 clients and 50 end-users. The insight was consistent: NovaPay was trusted, reliable, and fast — but nobody *felt* that when they looked at the product.

### Phase 1: Brand Foundation
We rebuilt the brand from the ground up — new wordmark, type system, color palette, and iconography. The new identity communicates precision and confidence without sacrificing warmth.

### Phase 2: Product Redesign
With the brand locked, we redesigned the core product flows — onboarding, dashboard, transactions, and settings. Every screen was designed in Figma and handed off with a component library of 200+ elements.

### Phase 3: Design System
The final deliverable was a fully documented design system, complete with usage guidelines, do's and don'ts, and Figma tokens mapped to production CSS variables.

## The Outcome

- 7× revenue growth in 14 months post-launch
- 42% reduction in onboarding drop-off
- Enterprise clients increased from 3 to 19
- Design system adopted by 6-person product team within 2 weeks of handoff
