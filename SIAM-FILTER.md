# SIAM Decision Filter — HS108 Design OS

This file is referenced by CLAUDE.md.
Run every design decision through these filters before shipping.

---

## FOUR PRIMARY QUESTIONS

Apply to every visual, layout, and interaction decision.

**1. Does this reduce or add cognitive load?**
A design element that makes the audience work harder to understand it is wrong.
Reduction is not minimalism — it is responsibility.

**2. Is this serving function or performing expression?**
Expression is allowed when it strengthens the brand's primary signal.
If it exists to impress or showcase creativity — it is misaligned.

**3. Would this feel right if no one noticed it?**
The strongest decisions feel inevitable. They disappear into recognition.
If the value depends on being noticed as clever, the decision is suspect.

**4. Does this improve the whole page or only the local moment?**
A component that looks good in isolation but breaks the system is wrong.
Every decision is evaluated against the whole.

---

## ENERGY DEFAULTS

Unless the brief explicitly demands otherwise:

- **Quiet over loud.** The brand does not compete for attention. It earns it.
- **Slow over fast.** Pacing respects cognitive load.
- **Grounded over expressive.** Stability builds trust faster than novelty.
- **Restrained over bold.** Boldness must earn its place. Restraint does not need justification.

---

## HIERARCHY

Level 1 — Primary signal (logo / type / color — whichever was declared). Must be unmistakable.
Level 2 — Structural information. Hierarchy, spacing, grid. Should feel obvious, not explained.
Level 3 — Brand character. Tone, personality, expressiveness. Must not interfere with Levels 1–2.
Level 4 — Detail and delight. Micro-refinements. Only after Levels 1–3 are stable.

Under time or budget pressure: sacrifice Level 4 first, Level 3 second. Never Level 1.

---

## HARD REFUSALS — never ship these

- Decorative complexity that exists to look interesting
- Trend-first decisions (what's working for other brands right now)
- Designs that require explanation to feel right
- Performative boldness — exaggerated contrast, dramatised motion, amplified emotion
- Fake authenticity — simulated roughness, decorative imperfection
- Premature polish over unresolved fundamentals

---

## FOR HS108 SPECIFICALLY

New build = Emerging → Stabilising stage.
- Clarity is mandatory. Polish is secondary.
- Functional truth outranks aesthetic refinement.
- If forced to choose between a beautiful system that is slightly confusing and a plain system that is unmistakable — choose unmistakable.
- Delight is earned after the fundamentals are locked.
